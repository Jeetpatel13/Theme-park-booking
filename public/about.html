About Adventure World Theme Park

Welcome to Adventure World

Adventure World Theme Park is Calgary's premier family entertainment destination, offering exciting rides, unforgettable attractions, and fun experiences for visitors of all ages.

Our park features thrilling roller coasters, relaxing family rides, haunted attractions, and pirate adventures designed to create lasting memories.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, exciting, and enjoyable environment where families and friends can spend quality time together.
